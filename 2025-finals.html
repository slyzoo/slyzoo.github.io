1900s Space Race

The start of the coined Space Race started when the United States declared to launch the first artificial satellite into space on july 9th, 1955. Only eight years into the cold war. The Russians had more frequency at the beginning and a total of about 48 missions. The Americans had about 27 missions total and had a frequency about half or even lower than the Russians.

Technology
