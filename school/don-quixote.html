Part 1 Summary

Alonso Quixano is a middle aged man, who lives in an unspecific area of La Mancha with his niece and housekeeper. Mr. Quixano reads so many chivalric romances, he goes mad renaming himself to 'Don Quixote'; renames his workhorse 'Rocinante' and is so delusional he renames a slaughterhouse worker, Aldonza Lorenzo, to Dulcinea del Toboso, his "lady love". He travels in search of adventure. he goes to an inn believing its a castle proceeds to call the prostitues "ladies", he calls the inn keeper "lord" and tries to convince the "lord" to knight Don Quixote. The inn keeper decides to play along and knights this delusional fellow. Don Quixote soon begins holding vigil at the castles chapel, which is actually the inn's horse trough.
